TECHQUEST


T.Q in short, is a platform where different people especially youth will be discussing technological ideas of different kinds. This platform will be on a specific chosen social media platform and will only be for Malawian youth so as to encourage people with talents in technology to continue.


People will be coming with their ideas and other people will be complementing and/or adding other things to the idea. This will make everyones idea come together and produce one thing.


Even though technology means applying mechanical and applied sciences to industrial use, even programming(coding) will be part of the hub. Not only that but also hacking(the good one though)
